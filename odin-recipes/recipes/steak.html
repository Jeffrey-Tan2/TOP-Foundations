medium-rare steak

Steak

Description

This recipe is for a medium-rare steak and is created by Jamie Oliver. It focuses on Jamie's favourite steak cut, the feather steak (also known as flat iron steak). Full of beautiful flavour, texture and fat marbling, feather steak is a good size to leave you satisfied

Ingredients

  • Feather steak
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Rub steak with olive oil and pinch of salt and pepper
  2. Add steak to a hot pan, cook for 6 minutes, turning every minute
  3. Add half a garlic clove and rub it over the steak every time you turn it
  4. Rub the steak with a knob of butter
  5. Rest steak for 2 minutes before eating to seal the juices
